FAQ

Do you travel outside Las Vegas?+/-

Yes! Local travel inside the Vegas Valley is included. We can travel further for a small mileage fee.

Can we combine DJ and Karaoke?+/-

Absolutely. Many events start with DJ sets and switch to karaoke later—or we can mix throughout.

What power and space do you need?+/-

A standard outlet (15A) within 50 ft and roughly a 10'x8' area works for most setups.

Are you insured?+/-

Yes. We carry general liability insurance and can provide a COI upon request.
